--- title: Homelessness Reduction Tech icon: fa-th order: 2 --- Welcome to the Reduce Austin Homelessness website, a application based effort to address the Homelessness issue in Austin, Texas. This effort is based on 3 separate but connected (by dataset) mobile / desktop apps. The 1st is a Homeless Services Locator. The 2nd is Homeless Activity Reporter. The 3rd is a Austin Homeless Activity Dashboard
